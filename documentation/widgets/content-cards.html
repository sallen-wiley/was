Widgets

Content cards

Presenting various types of content in a range of configured feeds

Throughout the Wiley Analytical Science site, variouse types of content ranging from articles to webinars, presents itself in content 'feeds'; such as latest news, upcoming events, or featured articles. This content is displayed with a flexible widget that identifies the content type and then presents the relative information in a 'card'. The cards are fluid elements that can stretch to fill the width of the container and expand downwards to fit the required information.

Cards are used in the following locations:

  • Content bands on homepages (WAS, topics)
  • Search results
  • Related content widgets

Card Anatomy

Type

The content type is indicated in the top left of the card. A card can only have one content type. For example 'Article' or 'News'.

The content type should not display if all cards within the content feed are configured to be the same content type. Example, The WAS home has a section titled 'Latest Articles', all content within this widget should be of the article type and therfore it would be redundant to repeat the content type within each card.

Topics

We display the associated topics to the right of the content type. We are proposing that Authors and Editors never assign more than 3 topics to a piece of content.

  • Individual topics composed of multiple words cannot wrap. Otherwise adjacent topics would run into eachother.
  • If the container is not wide enough, topics can wrap below eachother.
  • Subjects should not wrap below the content type.
  • We display all related topics on the following page types and in the following widgets:
    • WAS home
    • Search results
    • Related content widgets
  • We display all T2 topics related to the parent T1 topic on T1 topic homepages. Eg. Microscopy home
    • Note that it is possible for a piece of content that only has a T1 topic assigned would not show any topics in its card on this type of page
  • We do not display topics on T2 homepages. Eg. Light Microscopy home

Title

Titles act as links to the content view and should use a call-to-action colour. The follow rules apply to determine the title color:

  • Titles within cards on the WAS homepage are rendered in Burgandy
  • Titles within cards on the T1 and T2 topic homepages should be rendered in that topic's color. 
    • Microscopy
    • Spectroscopy
    • Separation Science
    • Equipment
    • Lab Automation
  • Title within cards on search results pages are rendered in Burgandy
  • Titles within cards in related content widgets are rendered in Burgandy

Snippet

Snippets are short passages of text that give a little more information about the content.

  • Snippets are shown on:
    • WAS and topic homepages
    • Search results
  • Snippets are not shown in:
    • Related content widgets

Author or Publication

Content can have authors and/or publications associated to them.

A content card can show either a publication or an author. Not both.

If a piece of content has an associated publication and authors, we only show the title of the publication.

If there is no associated publication, we show the first author. If there are multiple authors, we show the name of the first author and append 'et al.'.

If there is no publication or author, we show nothing in this space.

Publication Date

The date should render both the day and month the content was published.

If the content was published in a calendar year different to the current year, we also show that year.

Feature Image

  • Images in cards are cropped unless explicitely stated otherwise
  • Images in vertical cards can have a maximum height of 300px
  • Images in horizontal cards should have their height constrained by the height of the copy
