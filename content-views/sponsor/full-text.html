About

The Nanoscale and Microscale Research Centre (nmRC) is pleased to support this symposium, celebrating the exciting and wide-ranging applications of Raman Microscopy and Spectroscopy.

Across the physical and life sciences to the arts and humanities, Raman spectroscopy has become an essential multidisciplinary tool for the broadest spectrum of analysis. It has been used for determining chemical and structural changes in advanced nanoscale materials, monitoring the release of the next generation of drug molecules into the human body and authentication of precious gemstones and minerals. And did you know that the technique has been used in the analysis of ancient manuscripts?
